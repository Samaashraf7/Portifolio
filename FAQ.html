  • What do you do as a front-end developer?

    I specialize in building the visual and interactive parts of websites — everything users see and interact with. This includes layout, design, responsiveness, animations, and functionality using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React.

  • Can you make my website mobile-friendly?

    Absolutely! I build responsive websites that look great and function perfectly on all screen sizes — from phones to tablets to desktops.

  • Do you design the website too, or just developit?

    I can do both! If you already have a design (like from Figma or Adobe XD), I can turn it into code. If not, I can help create clean, modern designs tailored to your brand.

  • Will my website be fast?

    Yes! Performance is a top priority. I optimize images, code, and loading strategies to ensure your site runs quickly and smoothly.

  • How long will it take to build my website?

    It depends on the size and complexity of the project. A simple site might take a week, while more complex projects can take 2 ‐ 4 weeks or more. I'll give you a clear timeline after we discuss the details.

  • What technologies do you use?

    I primarily use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Tailwind, and sometimes Next.js. I'm always up to date with the latest tools to make your site modern and scalable.

  • Do you offer ongoing support omaintenance?

    Yes, I offer support packages if you need help with updates, improvements, or bug fixes after launch.
