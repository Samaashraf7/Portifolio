About Me

About Me

Who am i?

My name is Sama Ashraf, a 16 years old Front-End Developer & Web Designer passionate about crafting sleek, user-focused digital experiences, I study IT at ICTIA as a 10th grader.

What I Do : Build responsive, interactive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.
I work as a freelancer, helping businesses bring their ideas online and i worked as a freelancer for over 2 years now.
Currently Learning :-
Node.js, Express, and Databases to become a Full-Stack Developer

Based in: Shubra Masr, Cairo, Egypt

The Spark

It started with curiosity.
I designed my first landing page in Figma, just for fun. The idea of turning imagination into something interactive hooked me instantly.

The Designer in Me

I began creating custom websites for local businesses—clean, user-focused designs that brought real brands to life.
Favorite tool back then: Adobe XD
Biggest lesson: A beautiful interface means nothing if users can't enjoy the experience.

Developer Awakens

The magic wasn't complete until I could make designs come alive with code.
Learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
Created my first fully responsive website.
Spent nights debugging (and weirdly enjoying it).

Going Pro

I turned passion into profession and started working as a freelancer, helping businesses and individuals build their online presence.

Delivered 10+ responsive websites for clients worldwide
Specialized in React, Tailwind CSS, and modern UI design
Designed custom branding + UI kits for startups
Focused on fast-loading, SEO-friendly, and user-centric websites

Every project taught me something new—about design, code, and people.

Future Plans

I'm not stopping at the front end. My next big leap?
Mastering Backend Development with Node.js & Express

Diving into APIs, Databases (MongoDB, PostgreSQL), and Authentication

Building full-stack applications that blend stunning UI with powerful functionality Vision: Become a Full-Stack Developer who crafts seamless, end-to-end web experiences.
